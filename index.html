Know More About The Author

Harshdeep Singh

Harshdeep Singh is an author, a personal mastery coach, an accidental – engineer, youngest in a family of 5 and father of his pet dog, Rio. At the age of 18 Harshdeep got to know about the field of personal development. Wanting to explore more, he started working in a direct selling firm where he worked with hundreds people and thus gained experience in marketing, sales, training, leadership and management. Since the age of 18 he was working on his transformation and self-improvement but at 22 he went into depression due to facing losses in his business, resurfacing of childhood traumas and relationship failure.

In wanting to emerge from his depression he started his journey of exploring things of how he can heal and transform himself which further led him to learn Neuro-linguistic Programming, Hypnotherapy, Body language, CBT, and led him to find his mission in life. Thus rediscovering his “inner badass”.

Harshdeep is on a mission to help 100,000 people take charge of their own lives by healing their inner traumas, improving their belief system and take massive actions so that they can have fulfilling lives, ultimately contributing in making the world a better place, together.
